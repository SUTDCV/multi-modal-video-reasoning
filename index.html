Introduction

The ultimate goal of AI is to approach human intelligence, and one of the most significant components of human intelligence is the power of reasoning. The power of reasoning is ubiquitous, since humans confront with flooded multi-model information continuously in their daily life, including texts, visions and interactions with surroundings and we humans leverage the power of reasoning to understand and analyze the information.
