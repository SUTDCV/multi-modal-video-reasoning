Multi-Modal Video Reasoning and Analyzing Competition

ICCV 2021 Workshop

Introduction

To promote the research development in multi-modal video understanding and reasoning area, we organize the 1st Workshop on Multi-Modal Video Reasoning and Analyzing Competition, in conjunction with ICCV 2021. In this workshop, we have 4 competition tracks:

  • Track 1: Video Question Answering. SUTD-TrafficQA is a comprehensive VideoQA benchmark focusing on traffic-event based video reasoning. We provide 6 challenging reasoning tasks with 10,080 real-world videos and 62,535 human-generated QA pairs. For more details, please refer to SUTD-TrafficQA for our CVPR2021 paper and dataset.
  • Track 2: Skeleton-based Action Recognition. UAV-Human is a comprehensive and challenging human behavior understanding dataset captured by a flying UAV, containing 16,288 skeleton sequences and 155 action categories. For more details, please refer to UAV-Human for our CVPR2021 paper and dataset.
  • Track 3: Fisheye-based Action Recognition. Our UAV-Human also provides video samples recorded by a fisheye sensor, which can capture wide angle of views yet brings challenges because of large distortions in the edge areas. For fisheye-based action recognition track, we also provide 16,288 skeleton sequences as well as 155 action categories. Further details please refer to our dataset page.
  • Track 4: Person Re-Identification. We also provide a person reid track with 11,805 frames containing 618 identities, with diversified viewpoints, scenarios and occlusions. Please refer to the dataset page for details.
